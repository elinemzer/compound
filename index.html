What is Compound?
Compound is an easy way to invest in real estate you already understand.
When you invest through Compound, you buy shares in a company called a REIT (REIT stands for "Real Estate Investment Trust").
How does it work?
These shares represent an ownership stake in a company, the same way that shares in Apple, Google, or Amazon represent a stake in those companies.
